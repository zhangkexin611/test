About HSBC

HSBC Holdings plc is one of the world's largest banking and financial services organizations. With a presence in more than 60 countries and serving around 40 million customers globally, HSBC is committed to being wherever our customers need us, whether they are in their home country or conducting business abroad.

The bank operates under the motto "The World's Local Bank," reflecting its unique position as a global institution with deep local expertise and understanding of the markets it serves.

History & Heritage

Founded in 1865 in Hong Kong, HSBC has grown from a regional Asian bank to one of the world's most prominent financial institutions. With over 150 years of banking excellence, HSBC has continuously adapted to serve the evolving needs of its customers while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and professionalism.
